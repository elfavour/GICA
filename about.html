About Us

Are we the right choice for you?
Let's find out.

The name Grace Instrument Christian Academy is a manifestation of a two-fold epiphany: deliverance and preservation all by the Grace of God. In the first part of the epiphany, Apostle Paul’s message - “I am what I am by the grace of God”- resonated as I yearned for deliverance of God’s people from bondage of materialism and other idols for which I saw two worshippers foolishly praying. In the vision, I rejoiced being God’s crusader as HIS grace was poured upon me. In the second part of the epiphany I was charged, “As Moses was called to deliver Israel out of Egypt so you are called to preserve God’s people in the land”.
This preservation of the Spirit, Mind, Body, and Soul requires God’s grace upon us as we bring souls back to God and deliver them from bondage. Grace Instrument Christian Academy is an instrument of praise, deliverance, and preservation, as we seek God’s mission to all nations:

  • Know God for yourself.
  • Love HIM and others.
  • Learn so that you may show yourself approved.
  • Serve God through worship, work and works, solidarity, and leading.
  • Motto

    • One mission to all nations: know, fear, accept, and lead.

  • Mission

    • Grace Instrument Christian Academy would be an internationally renowned high school with a mission to create Christ-like communities by providing quality Christian education and producing God fearing, Holy Spirit filled Christian leaders for this generation and beyond.

  • Vision

    • Grace Instrument Christian Academy’s vision is that everyone will know and fear God, accept Jesus Christ as their personal savior, be filled with and led by the Holy Spirit, and lead others to HIM.

  • Core Values

    • Our core values are the foundation of who we are and dictate what we do. They will speak of our beliefs, responsibilities, commitments, expectations and goals. As a school, Grace Instrument Christian Academy’s core values are:

    • To Love.
    • To exercise our Faith.
    • To live in Purity.
    • To get knowledge.
  • Goals
    • Grace Instrument Christian Academy’s goals are to:
    • Retain highly qualified Christian teachers who are lifetime learners.
    • Equitable access to high quality education of all income levels.
    • Maintain a rigorous curriculum so that students are challenged.
    • Ensure that students are learning in a safe and enjoyable environment.
    • Provide an moral biblical basis to students to grow as leaders in Christ.
    • Assist students in learning by supporting all learning styles and levels.
    • Enrich students’ minds through culture (e.g. art, literature, music).
    • Instill healthy awareness in students for self, family, community, and world.
    • Challenge students to actively invest in own learning and pursue knowledge.
    • Utilize advanced technology to prepare students for the real world.
    • Promote wholesome and healthy lifestyles through exercise and living.
    • Provide education that edifies the intellect, spirit, social, emotional, and physical being of each individual.
    • Allow expression of every student’s creativity so as to promote growth.
    • Achieve full participation of all students, parents, faculty, and board.
    • Yield high academic achievement.
    • Hold all stakeholders accountable for student outcomes.
  • Philosophy

    • God, the Holy Spirit, and Jesus Christ guide Grace Instrument Christian Academy in all that we do and why we do it. God is the great “I AM”. He was, is, and will always be. God is omniscient, omnipotent, and omnipresent. He is the creator of Heaven and Earth and all that exists. God is love and can not lie. God is the giver of grace, mercy, and salvation. God charged us to be missionaries through education because HE wants us to grow other missionaries to bring people back to HIM. Therefore, we aim to:

    • to provide quality Christian education (modeling teachers).
    • to maintain certified, quality Christian teachers who are lifelong knowledge-seekers.
    • to raise God-fearing future leaders who seek to know Him.
    • to produce Holy-Spirit filled Christians who yearn to fulfill the great commission.
    • to be an Internationally renowned high school.

  • Non-discrimination Statement
    • Grace Instrument Christian Academy admits students of any race, sex, color, creed, national and ethnic origin, and disability to all the rights, privileges, programs and activities, generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in the administration of its educational policies, admission policy, financial aid policy, athletic and other school administered programs.
