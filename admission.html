Academic Program

Our goal as an institutiom is to provide a solid education in a Godly environment to our students. Our motto- raising a new generation of God-feearing leaders- indicates our intention to launch future leaders that will change the future of Belize and the world around them. This is why GICA is intentional in meeting all aspects of possible growth- Academic, spiritual, emotional and physical.

GICA offers courses in major fields of study along with extracurricular activities are available for our students. GICA uses a mixed curriculum (Abeka and CXC).

Entry Qualifications

GICA administration reserves the right to determine the admission, enrollment and re-enrollment of its students at all times.

Entry Qualifications: New Students only.
  • Primary school graduation
  • Good academic grade
    (70% average)
Entry Qualifications: Transfer Students only.
  • Good academic grade
  • GPA of 2.5 or higher

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate
  • PSE Result(Photocopy)(New students only)
  • Passport Size Picture
  • Recommendation letter(Principal and 1 teacher)
  • Social Security card/Passport Page(Photocopy)
  • Transcript from last school attended(Transferee only)
  • Standard V, and VI Report Card(New Students only)
  • Non-refundable application/Registration fee(50)

Scholarships and Payment Plan
